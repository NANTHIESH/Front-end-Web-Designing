BATTLE GROUNDS MOBILE INDIA ( BGMI ):























































ABOUT:


Battlegrounds Mobile India is a player versus player shooter game in which up to one hundred players fight
in a battle royale, a type of large-scale last man standing deathmatch where players fight to remain the last alive.
Players can choose to enter the match solo, or as a small team of upto four.
These are the types of maps available in BGMI:

  • Erangel
  • Livik
  • Miramar
  • Shanok
  • Vikendi
  • Karakin
